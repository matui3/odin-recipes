Mango Lassi

Description

Mango Lassi is a creamy, frothy Indian yogurt drink blending ripe mangoes, yogurt, milk, and a touch of cardamom or saffron. This vibrant orange beverage balances the sweetness of summer mangoes with the tang of yogurt, creating a refreshing drink that doubles as a dessert. Traditionally served chilled in tall glasses, it's the perfect antidote to spicy curries or sweltering heat.

What sets it apart is its versatility—adjust the thickness with milk or ice, sweeten with honey or sugar, and garnish with mint or chopped nuts. Whether enjoyed for breakfast, as a snack, or alongside biryani, Mango Lassi embodies the essence of tropical indulgence with every sip.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup ripe mango (fresh or frozen), chopped
  • 1 cup plain yogurt (Greek or regular)
  • ½ cup milk (or water for a lighter version)
  • 2 tbsp honey or sugar (adjust to taste)
  • ½ tsp cardamom powder (optional)
  • 4-5 ice cubes (optional)
  • Garnishes:
    • Mint leaves
    • Chopped pistachios
    • A pinch of saffron strands (optional)

Steps

  1. Prep ingredients:
    1. Peel and chop 1 cup ripe mango (or use frozen mango chunks)
    2. Measure yogurt, milk, and sweetener
  2. Blend:
    1. Add mango, yogurt, milk, honey/sugar, and cardamom to a blender
    2. Blend until smooth (30-45 seconds)
  3. Adjust consistency:
    1. Add ice cubes for thickness/chill (blend again if needed)
    2. For thinner lassi, add 1-2 tbsp water/milk
  4. Serve:
    1. Pour into glasses
    2. Garnish with mint, pistachios, or saffron
  5. Enjoy immediately! (Stir before drinking if separated)
